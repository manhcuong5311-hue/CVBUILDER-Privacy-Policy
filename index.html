Effective: 16 May 2026 Updated: 16 May 2026 App: CV Builder (iOS)

Short version: CV Builder runs entirely on your device. We don't have an account system, we don't have a server, and we don't collect or transmit your résumé, photo, voice, or usage to us or to any third party.

1. Who we are

CV Builder is published by SamCorp, an independent developer (the "Developer", "we", "us"). The app is distributed solely through Apple's App Store for iOS / iPadOS devices.

2. Data we store on your device

The app saves the following locally, inside its private sandbox under your iCloud-backed Application Support directory:

  • The résumé documents you create (text, structure, template, accent color).
  • A profile photo if you add one.
  • Thumbnails of each résumé used for the library dashboard.
  • Your appearance preference (Light / Dark / System) and onboarding flag.
  • Your remaining PDF export credits and whether you've unlocked the lifetime "Unlimited" pack.
  • Per-résumé spatial layout — where in AR you placed it, scale, rotation.

These files never leave your device unless you explicitly share a PDF via the iOS Share Sheet.

3. Data we do not collect

  • No accounts, no sign-in, no email or password.
  • No analytics SDK. No advertising SDK. No crash-reporting SDK.
  • No tracking across other apps or websites (no IDFA usage).
  • No background uploads of any kind.

4. Device permissions

Camera

Used by the AR feature ("Spatial Workspace") to place your résumé on a real wall or table via ARKit. The video feed is processed entirely on device and never recorded or transmitted.

Photo Library

Used only when you tap "Choose photo" to set a profile picture. We read a single picked image at the moment you select it; we never access the rest of your library.

Microphone & Speech Recognition

Used by the Professional Summary form when you tap the dictation button. Apple's Speech Recognition framework converts your voice to text on-device. We never store, transmit or process the raw audio. See Apple's privacy policy for details.

5. Apple Intelligence (AI rewrite)

On iOS 26 or later with Apple Intelligence enabled, the "AI rewrite" controls call Apple's on-device language model to suggest a rewritten summary. The text stays on device. We do not operate any AI servers of our own.

6. In-App Purchases

PDF export credits and the "Unlimited" lifetime unlock are purchased through Apple StoreKit. Payments are handled entirely by Apple — we never see your payment method or Apple ID. We only receive a verified transaction to grant credits/unlock locally on your device.

7. Children's privacy

The app is rated 4+ and suitable for general audiences. Because we collect nothing, we don't knowingly process any personal data of children. Parents can disable in-app purchases through Apple's Screen Time controls.

8. Data retention & deletion

Everything is stored locally. Delete a résumé anytime from the library. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. In-app purchases are tied to your Apple ID and can be restored with "Restore Purchases".

9. Your rights

We hold no personal data, so there is no account to delete or profile to export. Your device is the single source of truth. Contact us with questions and we will respond within 30 days.

10. International users

Processing takes place exclusively on your device, so no cross-border transfer of personal information happens through us. Purchases are processed by Apple in your local App Store region.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the app changes. The "Updated" badge above reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will also be called out inside the app.
